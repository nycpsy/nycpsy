Olivier Létang

Issues at work or in a relationship are snowballing and you are having a hard time getting a handle on them. Anxiety is becoming unmanageable. Or depression is taking hold. You need help to land on your feet.

Without devaluing emotional hardship, I believe symptoms can be viewed as opportunities. As a message that something needs to change. That your life, as it played out so far, cannot continue the same way, without reflection on the dark, hidden places.

It is in those places that lay the source of the painful repetitive patterns that often make us feel trapped in a life we didn't choose.

In my practice you will find a safe place where you can stop and take a deep look at your life and your own self. How did you get to feel what you feel now?

Being psychodynamically oriented, I aim for meaningful and lasting change and find myself guarded when faced with standardized approaches. It is my deep belief that your therapy should be specific to you . Together, we’ll look for patterns, identify the underlying core issues and systematically address those.

In this process, we’ll help you transcend those hidden impasses and get you back in a healthier place. A place where you can make good choices about how to live and how to find happiness and creativity that befits you.
