About | Explore Personal Growth - Schedule a Consultation — Olivier Létang

First trained in theoretical economics under the right hand of a Nobel winner, I spend a number of years in related fields.

The contrived human relationships of the corporate world left me wanting for a deeper, more meaningful engagement. I gravitated towards a deeper understanding of human relations and trained as a psychoanalyst with supplemental CBT skills.

My training lead me through NYU, NPAP and Columbia University. And I’m a member of various professional organisations and supervision groups.

In my practice, I help people with problems that feel deeply perplexing as well as people with issues that are very specific - a relationship/job problem, repetitive thoughts and rituals, addiction.

Being an immigrant, I am deeply attuned to acculturation issues, mixed heritages and Third Culture individuals. I also have experience with hearing limitation assimilations.

For your therapy to work, it is important for you to feel comfortable with your therapist.

Please reach out for a free initial consultation to see if we're a good fit.
